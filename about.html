ABOUT US

Our mission to give businesses a lighter path to funding.

We believe finance should give businesses the freedom to grow, not the burden of stress. Our goal is to simplify the process, help businesses make smarter choices, and create outcomes they can feel confident about. When finance works the right way, it changes everything.

We started Lighter Finance because business owners deserve a broker genuinely in their corner — one who knows what lenders want to see and how to get applications across the line. Not one chasing the highest commission.
